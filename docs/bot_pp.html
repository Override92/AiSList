Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

1 Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the AiSList Review Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the Bot, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

2 Data Controller

The AiSList project is maintained by community volunteers. For privacy inquiries, please contact us via:

  • GitHub: https://github.com/Override92/AiSList
  • Discord: AiSList Community Server

3 Data We Collect

3.1 Information Collected Automatically

When you interact with the Bot, we automatically collect:

Data Description
Discord User ID Your unique Discord identifier
Discord Username Your current Discord username
Server Nickname Your nickname on the Discord server (if set)
Interaction Timestamps When you use the Bot

3.2 Information You Provide

When submitting a channel, we collect:

Data Description
Channel Handle The YouTube channel you submit
Evidence URL The video URL you provide as evidence
Submission Details Any additional information included

3.3 Information for Maintainers

If you are a maintainer approving/rejecting submissions:

  • Approval/Rejection Actions - Which submissions you processed
  • Action Timestamps - When you processed submissions

4 How We Use Your Data

4.1 Core Functionality

  • Processing channel submissions
  • Notifying you about submission status
  • Managing the review workflow

4.2 Statistics and Analytics

We use your data to generate public statistics and analytics.

  • Generating contributor leaderboards
  • Tracking submission volumes and trends
  • Creating public statistics about blocklist growth
  • Displaying charts and analytics on our website

4.3 Abuse Prevention

  • Enforcing rate limits
  • Detecting and preventing spam or abuse
  • Identifying patterns of false submissions

4.4 Service Improvement

  • Understanding how the Bot is used
  • Improving the submission and review process

5 Data Sharing

5.1 Public Information

The following information may be publicly visible:

  • Your Discord username on contributor leaderboards
  • Submission counts attributed to your username
  • Your username in GitHub issue comments (if submission creates an issue)

5.2 Third Parties

We share data with:

  • GitHub - Submissions may create GitHub issues containing submission details
  • Discord - The Bot operates on Discord's platform

We do not sell your data to third parties.

6 Data Storage

6.1 Where Data is Stored

  • Submission data is stored on our servers and GitHub repositories
  • Discord-related data is processed through Discord's infrastructure and our servers

6.2 Retention Period

Data Type Retention
Submission data Indefinitely (part of blocklist history)
Rate limit data 1 hour
Interaction logs Up to 30 days

7 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

7.1 Access

Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

7.2 Correction

Request correction of inaccurate data.

7.3 Deletion

Request deletion of your data, subject to limitations:

  • We cannot delete data that has been merged into public GitHub repositories
  • Historical submission records may be retained for integrity purposes

7.4 Objection

Object to certain processing of your data.

7.5 Data Portability

Request your data in a portable format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us via GitHub or Discord.

8 Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure HTTPS connections
  • Access controls for maintainer functions
  • Regular review of security practices

However, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

9 Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

10 International Users

The Bot may be accessed globally. By using the Bot, you consent to the transfer of your data to servers that may be located outside your country of residence.

10.1 European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area:

  • Our legal basis for processing is legitimate interest (providing the service) and consent
  • You have additional rights under GDPR as described in Section 7
  • You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

11 Cookies and Tracking

The Bot itself does not use cookies. However, our website displaying statistics may use:

  • Essential cookies for functionality
  • Analytics to understand website usage

12 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13 Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests:

  • GitHub: https://github.com/Override92/AiSList/issues
  • Discord: AiSList Community Server

By using the AiSList Review Bot, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
